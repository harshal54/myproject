Aftercare

DG Bespoke Jewellery offers an annual maintenance service to ensure your jewellery is always in its best form and because beautiful jewellery deserves care and attention. Your piece of jewellery will be checked thoroughly to ensure precious gemstones have not worked loose through wear or accidental damage and the team will also clean and polish the piece. This service is always free of charge.
