DG Bespoke Jewellery

Hand-making fine jewellery since 1998

Combining the traditional methods my father taught me during my five year apprenticeship and the modern techniques of today, every piece is hand-made to perfection in my jewellery workshop which is situated on London’s famous Hatton Garden.

If you want to create something one off and special, I am able to work within all budgets.

I always source the best quality materials to ensure every piece of jewellery is not only stunning to you but a piece to be proud of making.
