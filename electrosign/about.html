Electro Signs and Design L.L.C., began in 1986 as Electro Neon and Design Inc. and has evolved to a full-service sign and lighting shop. We are a family run sign shop in Blaine that has been in business for over 30 years. We design, install, and manufacture custom signs, including interior and exterior signage as well merchandising, wayfinding and digital graphics including custom banners and more. We understand the dynamics of signage and are an experienced sign fabricator and installer. Electro understands brand awareness and the importance of visibility. Our seasoned team of professionals has successfully worked with cities, marketing managers, architects, designers, general contractors, and construction companies to meet signage expectations.
